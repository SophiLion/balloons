Meet Some of Our Crew

In ballooning, it is "the crew" who do most of the heavy physical labor of setting the balloon up, and packing it away again. Balloon Heaven's crew is highly experienced and passionate about providing our guests with a safe, fun flight. They are all members of the British Balloon and Airship Club BBAC and attend annual safety seminars with their pilot, attending classes tailored to the crew. Relax and enjoy the flight knowing you are in good hands with the Balloon Heaven Crew!

Sam Foley is in charge of our Ground Crew. Responsible for safe take-offs and landings and maintaining the balloons with his team members, Sam likes to play his guitar when he's not working. He can sometimes be persuaded to play for our guests.

Members of the Ground Crew preparing breakfast for our guests returning from the early morning ride over Wast Water and Windermere Lakes.

Jessica Bayley owns and operates Balloon Heaven with her husband Jeremiah, our head pilot. Jessica inherited the family business from her father twelve years ago. She is currently studying to pass her pilot exams. When she's not working or studying, Jessica is busy with her three children and Jeremiah.

Head Pilot Jeremiah Bayley who leads our Flight Crew has been flying for eighteen years. Hot air balloons are registered aircraft, the same as all fixed wing aircraft. The pilot in command must hold a valid Private Pilot's Licence PPL(B) issued by the Civil Aviation Authority specifically for ballooning.

Alison Farley is Head of Guest Services and she also oversees all of the bookings for private events.
