Exploring the Heavens in a Hot Air Balloon

Ballooning is not an individual sport. Our flight and ground crews are an essential component of a good and successful flight. Our crews are exceptionately well trained and passionate about the balloons. We are fortunate to have one of the most experienced teams in the United Kingdom, from our flying to our ground crew. Many of them winning awards for their exceptional ballooning skills.
